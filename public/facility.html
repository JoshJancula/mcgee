McGee Corporation delivers value with a state of the art 175,000SF manufacturing facility that enables quality control and precision to be engineered and manufactured into every product, every time.

Our unique manufacturing capabilities and dedicated, experienced employees enable your materials to be delivered on time, reducing onsite construction costs, and providing you with a quality finished product that will last for years.

McGee Corporation's leading edge manufacturing facility includes a fully automated steel fabrication line, shot blasting of all structural items for better paint adhesion, internal PVC drains and electrical conduits, aluminum gutters, certified welded connections, laser ACM Fascia panel routing, Stucco-Flex and custom architectural fascia designs, graphics application process.

Our engineering and fabrication capabilities are supported by a full-scale R&D department that provides our customers with the best products and services to meet their ever changing market demands.
